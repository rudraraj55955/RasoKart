typestringRequiredQR, Collect request or Intent link

Settlement Flow

Instant To Settlement

1

Customer scans QR / opens link

RasoKart creates a unique payment session for the merchant order.

2

Customer approves payment

Payment is completed inside the customer’s selected UPI app.

3

RasoKart processes transaction

Status, UTR, amount and merchant mapping are verified in the dashboard.

4

Webhook + settlement

Merchant receives callback and settlement label follows admin settings.
